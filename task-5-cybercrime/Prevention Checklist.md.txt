STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. OTP, UPI PIN or bank details are personal credentials. If Someone calls you and claim to be from your bank or a trusted service never share it to them.

2. Always verify payment requests before accepting them, especially on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe, as fraud person may send fake payment requests.

3. Avoid clicking on unknown links received through SMS, email, or social media, as they may lead to phishing websites.

4. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and enable two-factor authentication for security.

5. Do not install apps from unknown sources; always download applications from official app stores only .ex: In desktop you can link your chrome browser to McAfee like antivirus software.

6. Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi networks, especially when accessing banking or personal accounts.

7. Regularly check bank statements and transaction history to quickly identify any unauthorized activity.

8. Keep your phone and apps updated to ensure you have the latest security features and protections.

9. Do not trust calls or messages claiming urgent account issues without verifying through official customer care.

10. In case of any fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930.
